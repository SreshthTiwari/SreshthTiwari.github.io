About Me

I come from a remote village in Bihar, India. Growing up, I've spent most of my time helping out my uncle in his paddy farm, where I've learnt the importance of empathy and humility in collaboration. Hours of toiling in the sun have also instilled in me patience and perseverance.

As a member of the Dartmouth Student Government, I look forward to upholding these values while working my hardest to ensure a safe and inclusive environment on campus for every student.
